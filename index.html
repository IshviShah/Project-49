Interesting Facts About Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are Reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin is an important part of communication among chameleons
  3. Most Chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around three branches

Interesting Facts About Pigeon
